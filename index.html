My parents noticed a spark of interest in the game of basketball in me from a very young age. They noticed how my eyes lit up whenever I saw a basketball game on television or when I was playing pretend on the playground, dribbling an imaginary ball and making big shots in my head. It was clear to them that basketball brought me so much joy, and they could see the potential for me to develop a passion for the sport. Recognizing this enthusiasm, my parents made a thoughtful decision to buy me my own basketball. They believed that having a real ball would allow me to immerse myself more fully in the game, encouraging my imagination and giving me the opportunity to practice my skills. Every time I held that ball, I felt happy and excited, dreaming of the day I could play on a real court, like the players I admired. This simple gift marked the beginning of a journey that would shape my love for basketball and provide countless hours of joy and fun.

My father often reminisces about my childhood, sharing stories of how much I adored playing with a ball. From the moment I woke up in the morning until the time I went to bed at night, that ball was never far from my side. He recalls how I would carry it with me everywhere, whether I was in the backyard, at the park, or even inside the house. My parents would often buy me new balls, each one becoming a cherished companion in my daily adventures.

In this picture, I am transported back to the days when I played my very first game in a league. It was an exhilarating experience that marked the beginning of my journey into the world of basketball. I can still remember the excitement and nerves that filled me as I stepped onto the court for the first time, surrounded by teammates and my relatives.

In this photo, I was able to watch my idols play in live games for the first time, a moment I had been looking forward to for a long time. Attending these live events allowed me to immerse myself in the colorful atmosphere of basketball, and made me more aware of the intricacies and excitement of the sport. My interest in basketball deepened when I witnessed the skill, passion, and dedication of the players on the court. The experience was fun, and I felt so happy when I had the chance to take pictures with my idols. Meeting them in person was surreal; their presence inspired me in ways I never expected. From observing their techniques and strategies during the game, I learned valuable lessons that I can use in my own basketball journey. Every moment spent watching them strengthened my determination to improve my skills and strive for excellence in the sport. This experience not only deepened my love for basketball but also motivated me to work hard and pursue my dreams.

In this picture, you can see the place where my father first started encouraging me to get up early in the morning to play basketball. He always believed that starting the day with some physical activity would not only improve my skills but also boost my energy and mood. I remember a mixture of excitement and trepidation when I had the opportunity to participate in a 5v5 game with my friends. It was a thrilling experience, and I felt a great sense of camaraderie on the court. Looking back, I realize how much those mornings shaped my love for the game and the valuable lessons I learned about teamwork and perseverance.

This photo captures an unforgettable day when I had the incredible opportunity to watch my idols from the NBA at Fiba World Cup, including Karl-Anthony Towns and Jordan Clarkson, play live. The atmosphere in the arena was electric, and I felt great joy as I cheered for them from our seats, which were quite high. Despite the distance, the energy of the crowd and the skill of the players made it an unforgettable experience. Watching them in person gave me a deeper appreciation for their talent and hard work. I learned a lot from their style of play and how they interacted with each other on the court. It is truly inspiring to see these athletes perform at such a high level right here in the Philippines. I left the game feeling motivated and eager to improve my own skills, hoping that one day I could emulate their success.

In this photo, it captures the day I try out for the Varsity basketball team for the first time at Pamantasan ng Lungsod ng Pasig. This moment holds a special place in my heart because, for me, it represents my greatest achievement in playing basketball. As a shy person, stepping onto that court felt scary, but comforting. I've always admired the skill and confidence of the players I've watched, and now I'm jumping into that world myself. Leaving my comfort zone was not easy, but I was driven by my passion for the game and the desire to challenge myself. I remember a mixture of excitement and nervousness as I joined my teammates in tryouts, but I suppressed those feelings. My father was there to support me, and seeing his pride made the experience even more meaningful. I'm really happy that I made this move, and I hope to continue to grow as a player and as a person.
